VR Designer

Summary

The app would be a photoshop/adobe XD like app but will be accessed through a VR headset and Kinect. It allows users to edit pictures and design apps using their hands to move, resize, and edit different aspects of an image or app workflow. To use the app the user would need an Kinect ($99) and either google cardboard ($15-$30) or Samsung Gear VR ($50). The Kinect would track the users body movements and allow them to control their design with their hands. The app could be used in school art classes as a new medium for creating artwork.
